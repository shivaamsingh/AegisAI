# Problem Statement

## Background

Bridge infrastructure is critical to transportation networks. Traditional inspection methods rely on scheduled manual inspections, which may fail to detect structural deterioration between inspection cycles.

Environmental conditions such as rainfall, temperature changes, traffic load, and aging significantly affect bridge health.

---

## Problem

Existing maintenance systems are primarily reactive rather than predictive.

As a result:

* Damage may remain undetected
* Maintenance costs increase
* Public safety is affected
* Unexpected bridge closures occur

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-powered predictive infrastructure monitoring system capable of estimating bridge failure risk before significant deterioration occurs.

The system combines:

* Bridge information
* Weather data
* Traffic data
* Maintenance history

Machine learning models estimate the probability of structural deterioration and provide interpretable explanations.

---

## Objectives

1. Predict bridge risk.
2. Identify high-risk bridges.
3. Explain prediction results.
4. Recommend maintenance priorities.

---

## Expected Output

* Risk Score
* Failure Probability
* Maintenance Recommendation
* Interactive Dashboard
