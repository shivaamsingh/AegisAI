# AegisAI

## Overview

AegisAI is an AI-powered infrastructure monitoring and predictive maintenance platform that estimates the structural risk of bridges before failures occur. It combines infrastructure data, weather information, traffic patterns, maintenance history, and machine learning to support proactive maintenance decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Bridge inspections are usually periodic and reactive. Damage may go unnoticed between inspections, increasing the risk of structural failure, service disruption, and financial loss.

---

## Proposed Solution

AegisAI predicts bridge deterioration using multiple data sources and provides:

* Infrastructure risk prediction
* Explainable AI
* Maintenance recommendations
* Interactive visualization dashboard

---

## Objectives

* Predict bridge failure risk
* Detect high-risk bridges
* Explain prediction results
* Support maintenance planning
